Meet the team

The team that makes everything happen.

Mark Muriithi

Chief Operating Officer

Leading the company as the Co-Founder and CEO, Bonface oversees the company’s efforts to revolutionize the food supply Chain in the continent. With 7-year experience working in the African Tech scene, he brings a rich background experience to propel the business growth and expansion across the continent. Prior to starting Afriagrimark, Bonface was the Co-Founder and head of corporate partnership before transitioning to head of Business development. He oversaw the company’s growth across multiple Kenyan cities in one and a half years. Besides having a Bachelor’s degree in Food Science, he is both a Young African Leadership Fellow and Jasiri Talent Investor Fellow.
