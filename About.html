About us

TUSTOCK GROWERS is an agritech aggregator company that is revolutionizing the agricultural industry by empowering farmers and facilitating their growth. With a strong foundation of 5000 existing farmers, we are committed to providing innovative solutions and driving sustainable agricultural practices. Our mission is to bridge the gap between farmers and market opportunities, ensuring a prosperous future for both.

Vision Statement

At TUSTOCK GROWERS, our vision is to become a leading force in transforming the agricultural landscape, empowering farmers, and promoting sustainable farming practices. We aspire to create a vibrant ecosystem that connects farmers, investors, and market opportunities, ensuring food security, economic growth, and environmental sustainability.

Stevia Farming

Stevia farming is one of our flagship programs at TUSTOCK GROWERS. Stevia is a highvalue, low-calorie natural sweetener that is in high demand globally. Our stevia farming program aims to capitalize on this market demand by equipping farmers with the knowledge, resources, and support needed to cultivate stevia crops. With its numerous health benefits and growing consumer preference for natural sweeteners, stevia farming presents a lucrative opportunity for farmers to increase their income while meeting market demand sustainably.
