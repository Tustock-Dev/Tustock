Our Vision: Transforming Into an agritech led Company

TUSTOCK GROWERS is an agritech aggregator company that is revolutionizing the agricultural industry by empowering farmers and facilitating their growth. With a strong foundation of 5000 existing farmers, we are committed to providing innovative solutions and driving sustainable agricultural practices. Our mission is to bridge the gap between farmers and market opportunities, ensuring a prosperous future for both.

Transformation Transforming agricultural landscape through agritech.

Building Capability Empowering farmers and promoting sustainable farming practices.

Enhanced level of automation digitization Creating a vibrant ecosystem connecting farmers, investors, and market opportunities.

Food Security Triad Ensuring food security, economic growth, and environmental sustainability.

Flagship programs Stevia farming and French beans farming to drive sustainable crop cultivation.
