KUZA

Kuza is an innovative agricultural credit instrument, designed to finance farmer's equipment and extension services. It operates as a high yield bond with a low credit rating, providing farmers 15% interest cover for two planting and harvesting seasons, and a repayment tenure of up to 180 days. With a type BB- / Ba3 investment grade, Kuza welcomes banks and financial institutions seeking expansion opportunities.
Beyond financial assistance, Kuza empowers farmers by offering allocated bank accounts and comprehensive financial literacy programs. Its primary aim is to facilitate the transition to smart farming practices, boosting productivity, income, and program sustainability. With an open and inclusive approach, Kuza fosters collaboration and growth within the agricultural sector.

SMART farming practices

Our Smart agricultural practices align with the principles of non-revenue water, water conservation, and efficient irrigation ; they play a pivotal role in sustainable farming. By implementing advanced technologies and innovative strategies, farmers can optimize water usage, minimize wastage, and enhance overall irrigation efficiency. These practices encompass a range of approaches, including precision irrigation systems, drip irrigation, soil moisture sensors, and remote monitoring systems. Furthermore, farmers can adopt water-saving techniques such as mulching, rainwater harvesting, and using recycled or treated water for irrigation purposes. By integrating these smart agricultural practices, we can ensure the responsible and efficient use of water resources, minimize environmental impact, and promote a more sustainable future for agriculture.
